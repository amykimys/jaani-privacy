This Privacy Policy describes how Yunseo Kim ("we," "our," or "us") collects, uses, and protects your information when you use the Jaani mobile application (the "App").

Jaani is designed with privacy in mind. We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal information from users of our app.

What We Don't Collect

  • Personal Information: We do not collect names, email addresses, phone numbers, or any personally identifiable information.

  • Usage Analytics: We do not track how you use the app or collect usage statistics.

  • Location Data: We do not access or collect any location information.

Local Storage Only

All your data (todos, habits, preferences) is stored locally on your device. This means:

  • Data is not synced across devices
